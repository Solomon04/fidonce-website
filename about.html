Eric Evans · Founder & Head Coach

From All-American
to all-in on Chester.

After a standout career at Cheyney University — where he earned All-American honors — Eric Evans came home. Not to coach for a paycheck. To do the work nobody else would.

He took young athletes seriously when nobody else did. Drilled fundamentals until they were instinct. Treated character like a fundamental too. He started with his brother, Tyreke, on driveways and public courts across Chester. The training that produced an NBA Rookie of the Year wasn't lightning in a bottle — it was a system. And the system became FiDonce.

Today, FiDonce serves athletes from third grade through the pros. The names change. The standard doesn't. Every program — Children's Group, Intermediate, Elite, AAU, private — runs on the same foundation: development of the whole person.
